Frequently Asked Questions

What is solar energy? +
Solar energy is the energy harnessed from the sun using solar panels and other technologies to generate electricity or heat.
How do solar panels work? +
Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells, which generate direct current (DC) power that is converted to alternating current (AC) by an inverter.
What are the benefits of using solar energy? +
Solar energy is renewable, reduces electricity bills, has low maintenance costs, and helps reduce carbon footprint.
Is solar installation expensive? +
While the initial cost may seem high, government incentives and the long-term savings on energy bills make it a cost-effective solution.
How long do solar panels last? +
Most solar panels have a lifespan of 25-30 years, with many still operating efficiently beyond that period.
Do solar panels work during cloudy days? +
Yes, solar panels can still generate electricity on cloudy days, though their efficiency may be reduced compared to sunny days.
Can solar energy power an entire house? +
Yes, with a properly sized system and battery storage, solar energy can power an entire house and meet its energy needs.
Do I need to clean my solar panels? +
Solar panels require minimal maintenance, but occasional cleaning may be needed to remove dirt or debris for optimal performance.
Are solar panels environmentally friendly? +
Yes, solar panels generate clean energy, reduce reliance on fossil fuels, and have a smaller environmental impact than traditional energy sources.
What happens to excess energy generated by solar panels? +
Excess energy can be stored in batteries or sent back to the grid, depending on your system setup and local regulations.
Can I install solar panels on any type of roof? +
Most types of roofs can support solar panels, but the roof's condition, angle, and material may affect installation suitability.
